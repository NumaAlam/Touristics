========================================
 LOWER AUSTRIA TOURIST PORTAL — HELP
 Quick Reference for Hotel Representatives
========================================

For full step-by-step instructions, refer to the Tourist Company User Manual
provided by your NOE-TO contact.

----------------------------------------
VIEW MY HOTEL DATA
----------------------------------------
Displays your hotel's master record (name, address, category, rooms, beds).
To correct any details, select your hotel and click Edit. Review all fields,
tick the Confirmation checkbox, then click Save and confirm with Yes.
For changes you cannot make yourself, contact NOE-TO.

----------------------------------------
ADD TRANSACTION DATA
----------------------------------------
Enter your monthly occupancy figures. Select the year and month, then enter
the number of Used Rooms and Used Beds for that period.
Used Rooms and Used Beds cannot exceed your hotel's total Rooms and Beds.
Each month can only have one record — duplicates will be rejected.
Click Save to submit.

----------------------------------------
VIEW TRANSACTION DATA
----------------------------------------
Shows all occupancy records submitted for your hotel. Use the From/To Year
and Month dropdowns to filter by date range.
To correct an existing record, contact NOE-TO.

----------------------------------------
HELP
----------------------------------------
Displays this help text.

----------------------------------------
LOGOUT
----------------------------------------
Ends your session and returns to the Login screen. Always log out before
closing the application.
